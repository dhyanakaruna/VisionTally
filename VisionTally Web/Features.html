FEATURES

Our software is capable of counting anything with the right counting model.
A limitless amount is possible

Accuracy

Counting your inventory infrequently or manually can result in inaccurate counts. We believe that Vision Tally will help you get a fair count every single time.

Model Integration

The image processing of multiple images can be done via camera feeds or existing folders. Using the folders, verifying and quantifying are automated.

Database integration

You can customize the results by specifying product details, such as ID, category, and checklists Attributes (volume, diameter, Etc.) Depending on the user's preference, the data can be saved on their device or sent via email, or Uploaded to a database or to an external web service.

Background Elimination

Each pipes are identified and isolated from the background by clicking and dragging a particular region
