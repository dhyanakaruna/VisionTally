How it Works

Image processing techniques are high precision measurement systems that are frequently used in production lines. With these contactless control and measurement, production facilities can reach high production numbers without errors. Image processing operations such as product counting, error control, dimension measurement can be performed through a camera that can perform object-independent product counting. In this approach using images obtained from a real experimental setup, a real-time machine vision application was installed. As a result of the experimental studies performed, it has been determined that the proposed approach gives fast, accurate, and reliable results.
